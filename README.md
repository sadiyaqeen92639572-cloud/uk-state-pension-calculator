# UK State Pension Calculator 2026

Free British state pension calculator — enter date of birth and NI qualifying years to get your state pension age, exact retirement date, and weekly pension estimate.

**Live site:** [state-pension-calculator.co.uk](https://state-pension-calculator.co.uk)

## Features

- State pension age by birth year (66, 67, 68)
- NI years → weekly / monthly / annual pension estimate (2025/26 rates: £230.25/week full pension)
- pSEO pages for birth years 1955–1979
- Satellite calculators: NI gaps, voluntary contributions, deferral, self-employed, divorced women, WASPI
- JSON-LD structured data (WebApplication, FAQPage, HowTo, BreadcrumbList)
- sitemap.xml with 29 URLs
